## Summary of concepts

- AI/ML systems introduce a different attack surface from traditional web applications.
- Prompt-based systems can be manipulated in ways that feel different from classic vulnerabilities, but still revolve around trust, control, and unsafe input handling.
- Prompt engineering and prompt injection are useful to study because they affect how LLM-based systems behave under adversarial input.

---

## Deeper understanding (optional but recommended)

One thing that stands out with AI/ML security is that the “input” is not just data in a normal sense - it can directly influence model behavior.

That makes these systems interesting because:
- they still have trust boundaries
- they still have unsafe assumptions
- but the attack surface feels more behavioral than traditional memory corruption or backend injection
